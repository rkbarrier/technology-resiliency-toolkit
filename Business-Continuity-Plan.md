# Business Continuity Plan

## Executive Overview

This Business Continuity Plan (BCP) provides a structured framework for maintaining critical business operations during and after a disruptive event. It outlines governance, recovery priorities, communication procedures, and operational strategies that help minimize business disruption while protecting customers, employees, and organizational assets.

> **Portfolio Note:** This document is an original example created for professional portfolio purposes. It demonstrates business continuity planning concepts and does not contain confidential or proprietary information from any employer.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this Business Continuity Plan is to ensure that critical business services can continue operating or be restored within acceptable timeframes following an operational disruption.

---

# Business Continuity Objectives

* Protect employees and customers.
* Maintain delivery of critical business services.
* Reduce operational downtime.
* Minimize financial and reputational impact.
* Support regulatory and policy compliance.
* Enable timely recovery of business operations.

---

# Business Continuity Governance

| Role                        | Responsibilities                                                 |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Executive Sponsor           | Provides strategic direction and authorizes continuity decisions |
| Business Continuity Manager | Coordinates continuity planning and response                     |
| Technology Recovery Lead    | Supports restoration of technology services                      |
| Business Unit Leaders       | Execute department continuity procedures                         |
| Communications Lead         | Coordinates internal and external communications                 |
| Risk Management             | Monitors operational and regulatory impacts                      |

# Critical Business Functions

## Priority 1

* Customer-facing services
* Enterprise payment operations
* Authentication and access management
* Core business applications

## Priority 2

* Reporting and analytics
* Vendor management
* Internal operational support

## Priority 3

* Administrative functions
* Training and development
* Non-critical business activities

# Continuity Strategies

* Cross-trained personnel

# Testing & Maintenance

Business continuity plans should be:

* Reviewed annually
* Updated following organizational changes
* Tested through tabletop and operational exercises
* Revised after significant incidents
* Approved by business leadership

---

# Success Measures

* Business continuity plans remain current.
* Critical business services meet recovery objectives.
* Recovery exercises achieve established goals.
* Stakeholders understand their responsibilities.
* Improvement opportunities are documented and implemented.
