# Business Impact Analysis (BIA)

## Project Overview

This Business Impact Analysis (BIA) demonstrates a structured approach to evaluating critical business services, identifying operational dependencies, and establishing recovery priorities following a disruptive event.

> **Portfolio Note:** This is an original example created for professional portfolio purposes and does not contain confidential or proprietary information.

---

## Business Service

Commercial Payment Processing

---

## Business Purpose

Provide secure, accurate, and timely processing of commercial payment transactions while supporting customer access, regulatory compliance, and operational continuity.

---

## Critical Business Functions

* ACH Payment Processing
* Domestic Wire Transfers
* International Wire Transfers
* Payment Exception Handling
* Customer Payment Support
* Settlement Processing

---

## Business Impact if Unavailable

### 0–4 Hours

* Payment delays
* Increased customer inquiries
* Manual workarounds initiated

### 4–24 Hours

* Growing transaction backlog
* Operational inefficiencies
* Increased financial and reputational risk

### 24–72 Hours

* Significant customer impact
* Regulatory reporting concerns
* Potential financial loss
* Executive incident management required

---

## Recovery Objectives

| Objective                      |     Target |
| ------------------------------ | ---------: |
| Recovery Time Objective (RTO)  |    4 Hours |
| Recovery Point Objective (RPO) | 15 Minutes |
| Maximum Acceptable Outage      |   24 Hours |

---

## Key Dependencies

### Technology

* Payment Processing Platform
* Authentication Services
* Database Infrastructure
* Network Connectivity
* Monitoring & Alerting

### Business

* Operations Team
* Treasury Operations
* Customer Support
* Technology Support
* Vendor Partners

---

## Recovery Strategy

* Activate incident response procedures
* Restore critical infrastructure
* Validate application functionality
* Resume payment processing
* Reconcile outstanding transactions
* Communicate status to stakeholders
* Conduct post-incident review

---

## Risk Considerations

* Infrastructure failure
* Cybersecurity incidents
* Third-party service disruption
* Data integrity issues
* Staffing shortages

---

## Success Measures

* Recovery completed within RTO
* No material data loss
* Critical payment services restored
* Customer communications delivered on schedule
* Successful post-recovery validation
