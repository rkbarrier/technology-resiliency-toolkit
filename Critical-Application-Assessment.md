# Critical Application Assessment

## Executive Overview

This Critical Application Assessment demonstrates a structured approach to identifying, evaluating, and prioritizing technology applications based on their importance to business operations. The assessment supports technology resiliency planning, disaster recovery, business continuity, and operational risk management.

> **Portfolio Note:** This document is an original example created for professional portfolio purposes. It demonstrates enterprise resiliency assessment concepts and does not contain confidential or proprietary information from any employer.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this assessment is to identify critical technology applications, evaluate their business impact, establish recovery priorities, and support informed resiliency planning.

---

# Assessment Criteria

Applications are evaluated using the following criteria:

* Business Criticality
* Customer Impact
* Operational Impact
* Financial Impact
* Regulatory Impact
* Recovery Complexity
* Technology Dependencies
* Third-Party Dependencies

# Risk Assessment

| Risk                   | Impact | Mitigation                             |
| ---------------------- | ------ | -------------------------------------- |
| Infrastructure Failure | High   | Redundant infrastructure               |
| Vendor Outage          | High   | Alternate provider strategy            |
| Data Corruption        | High   | Backup validation and recovery testing |
| Cybersecurity Incident | High   | Incident response procedures           |
| Staffing Constraints   | Medium | Cross-training and succession planning |

---

# Recovery Prioritization

## Priority 1

Applications required to restore customer-facing services and critical business operations.

## Priority 2

Applications supporting operational effectiveness and reporting.

## Priority 3

Applications supporting administrative and non-critical business activities.

---

# Recommendations

* Review application criticality annually.
* Validate RTO and RPO objectives with business stakeholders.
* Test recovery procedures on a regular schedule.
* Monitor third-party dependencies.
* Update application inventories following major technology changes.

---

# Success Measures

* Critical applications have documented recovery strategies.
* Recovery objectives are approved by business owners.
* Recovery testing validates operational readiness.
* Technology dependencies are documented and maintained.
* Recovery documentation is reviewed and updated annually.
